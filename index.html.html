Project 01

Sacred Space

Trauma Recovery Coach — Instagram & Facebook

The Challenge

A trauma recovery coach with deep expertise and a powerful message — but a social media presence that wasn't doing her justice. Generic motivational quotes, no visual identity, no content strategy. Her audience couldn't tell what she did, who she served, or why they should trust her.

❌ Before

What we were working with

  • Random motivational quotes with no connection to her brand
  • Inconsistent colors, fonts and visual style across both platforms
  • No highlights — visitors had no way to understand her offers
  • Generic captions with no clear voice, niche or call to action
  • No content pillars — posts felt scattered and purposeless
  • Bio wasn't converting — unclear who she helped or how

📸 Before

→
✅ After

What we built together

  • Defined 4 clear content pillars rooted in trauma recovery and healing
  • Created a cohesive visual identity — consistent palette, typography & aesthetic
  • Redesigned and wrote all Instagram Highlights: About, Services, Testimonials, Free Resources & FAQ
  • Rewrote both bios to speak directly to her ideal client with a clear CTA
  • Shifted caption style to warm, empathetic, authority-led storytelling
  • Built a consistent posting strategy across Instagram & Facebook
  • Content that positioned her as a credible, trusted expert — not just another quote page

✨ After

My Process

01
Audit & Discovery

Deep-dived into her existing content, audience, and the language her ideal clients use when they're searching for healing.

02

Project 02

Synergy in Bloom

Mental Health Coaches Community — Social Media Management

The Brief

A community built for mental health coaches needed a social media presence that matched the depth of its mission — one that could build trust, spark real conversations, and warm up an audience before programs and coaching offers were ever introduced.

What I Did

01
Built from the Ground Up

Established the brand's entire social media presence from scratch — creating clarity, visual consistency, and a tone of voice that felt safe, credible, and aligned with a mental-health-focused audience.

02
Community-First Content Strategy

Developed and executed a strategy built around meaningful engagement over vanity metrics — increasing follower growth while fostering real connection and conversation within the community.

03
Value-Driven Content Creation

Created highly engaging content designed to educate, resonate emotionally, and speak directly to the challenges mental health coaches face — building trust long before any offer was made.

04
Pre-Launch Audience Preparation

Collaborated on pre-launch content planning to ensure the audience was warm, curious, and ready — so when coaching programs and offers launched, the community responded with interest and trust already in place.
